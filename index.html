About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm privileged enough to be a creative and a computer engineering student, so that I can at least try to make my ideas come to life. I was born in the Philippines and moved to Hawaiʻi at a young age. From the GameBoy Advanced and PSVitas to Toshiba laptops and eventually my own Desktop, I remember being around computers and tech for as long as I can remember.

Besides studying or working on personal projects, I enjoy a fair amount of drawing, exercise, and graphic design. I also very much love collaborating with others and learning new perspectives, which is why I'm passionate about video games and traveling—lots of perspectives and teamwork involved. Personally doing my best to be open-minded and focusing on what I can learn/improve without taking myself too seriously.

I'm an avid user of Adobe Photoshop for graphic design and illustration. Familiar with Python, POSIX C, C++, and HTML. Well-versed in certain Google services (Docs, Sheets, Slides) and Microsoft products (Word, Excel), with amateur experience in LaTeX.
